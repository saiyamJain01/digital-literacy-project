# 🛡️ Social Media Etiquette Guide for College Students

As a Student Digital Ambassador, I have compiled this checklist to help my peers navigate the digital world safely and responsibly.

| **Do's** | **Don'ts** |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Do** use strict privacy settings to control who can see your personal posts and data. | **Don't** post sensitive personal information like your home address, phone number, or OTPs. |
| **Do** verify the source of any news or academic information before sharing it to prevent misinformation. | **Don't** engage in cyberbullying, use offensive language, or post aggressive comments on public forums. |
| **Do** keep your professional profiles like LinkedIn and GitHub updated with your VIT Bhopal credentials. | **Don't** share photos or videos of others without their explicit permission. |
| **Do** use a professional and clear profile picture for any accounts used for networking and internships. | **Don't** overshare your real-time campus location or daily schedule for security reasons. |
| **Do** think before you post: "Would I be comfortable with a future recruiter or professor seeing this?" | **Don't** click on suspicious links, "free giveaway" ads, or unverified scholarship offers. |
